What is Safe Zone

Safe Zone is a lifesaving platform designed to help people navigate war zones and natural disasters by identifying dangerous areas, safe shelters, and aid distribution points. The website relies on real-time, crowdsourced reports from users to map out risk zones and highlight safe locations. It features an emergency button that instantly notifies the national helpline, ensuring quick access to assistance when needed. By integrating government alerts and NGO resources, Safe Zone provides reliable, up-to-date information to help individuals and communities stay safe during crises. Our mission is to empower people with critical information when it matters most.
